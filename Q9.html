Text Formatting In HTML.

  1. bold (b)
    • Use to attract an eye towards a specific point.
    • Avoid to used as a whole paragraph
    • Avoid repetation, as it can make text difficult to read and iritating
  2. italic (i)
    • Use to differentiate between words
    • Can be used for titles, foreign words or phrases, or to demonstrate thoughts or quotes within a sentence
    • Avoid repetation, as it can become iritating or lose its impact
  3. strong
    • Basically it's used for important or significant text
    • Also it can be used to highlight, warnings, or notices
    • Can be combined with other formatting tags, i.e. bold or italic.
  4. underline (u)
    • The content inside is typically displayed with an underline.
    • You can change this with the help of CSS.
    • Also it's an inline element.
